The Majestic Horse

Designed by previous 2017 Lego Masters UK winners Nathan Dias and Steve Guinness. Given to select members of the Recognised Lego Fan Media group for an April fools prank in 2023, only around 10 are known to exist. Although pictures show box art for this set, it was received in a clear plastic bag in a standard box. Building instructions were locked with a password and unprintable, so it will likely never be available to the public. Some sets were received with 4 red 25269, and some had 4 dark red 25269.
